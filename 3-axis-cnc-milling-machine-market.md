<p><p>The 3-axis CNC milling machine market can be categorized into three types: horizontal, vertical, and universal. Horizontal CNC milling machines have a horizontal spindle orientation, ideal for producing symmetrical parts and accessing multiple sides of the workpiece. Vertical CNC milling machines have a vertical spindle orientation, suitable for cutting complex shapes and drilling holes. Universal CNC milling machines combine the features of both horizontal and vertical machines, enabling flexibility in cutting various parts. Each machine type caters to different manufacturing needs, offering different advantages and applications in the industry.</p></p>
