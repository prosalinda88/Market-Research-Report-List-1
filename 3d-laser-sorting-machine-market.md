<p><p>The 3D laser sorting machine market comprises different types of machines, two of which are belt sorting machines and rotary disc sorting machines. Belt sorting machines use a conveyor belt system to transport and sort objects based on their optical properties detected by 3D laser sensors. On the other hand, rotary disc sorting machines rotate the objects on a disc, scanning them with 3D laser sensors to identify and separate them according to predetermined criteria. Both machines provide efficient and accurate sorting processes using 3D laser technology.</p></p>
