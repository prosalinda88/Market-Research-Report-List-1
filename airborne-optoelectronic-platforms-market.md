<p><p>The airborne optoelectronic platforms market includes different types of technologies, such as multispectral and hyperspectral imaging. Multispectral imaging uses sensors to capture data across a few specific wavelength bands, allowing users to gather information about different features in a scene. On the other hand, hyperspectral imaging captures data in many narrow and contiguous wavelength bands, providing a more detailed analysis of the scene's properties and enabling better identification and characterization of objects. Both technologies have diverse applications, including agriculture, environmental monitoring, surveillance, and defense.</p></p>
