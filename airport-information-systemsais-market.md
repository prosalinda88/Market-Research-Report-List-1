<p><p>Airport Information Systems (AIS) encompass various technologies and software solutions used in airports for efficient operations. The market for AIS can be categorized into Airport Operation Control Centers (AOCC) and Departure Control Systems (DCS). AOCC helps manage overall airport operations, including flight scheduling, resource allocation, and passenger services. DCS, on the other hand, focuses on managing departures specifically, handling passenger check-ins, boarding, and baggage processes. Both AOCC and DCS contribute to enhanced airport efficiency and passenger experience, driving the demand for AIS in the market.</p></p>
