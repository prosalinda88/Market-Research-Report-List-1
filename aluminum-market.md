<p><p>Aluminum market types include aluminum billets and foundry alloy ingots. Aluminum billets are semi-finished metal products that are hot-rolled or extruded and then reworked into various shapes and sizes for further processing. They are commonly used in the automotive, aerospace, and construction industries. Foundry alloy ingots, on the other hand, are used by foundries for the production of different aluminum castings. These ingots are carefully alloyed and cast to meet specific requirements, ensuring high quality and accurate composition in the final products.</p></p>
