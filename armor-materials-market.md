<p><p>The armor materials market is categorized into four main types. Aramid materials are synthetic fibers known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, commonly used in body armor and helmets. Composites are made by combining multiple materials, such as fiberglass and resin, resulting in lightweight yet strong armor. Metals and alloys, like steel and titanium, offer excellent protection due to their inherent strength and durability. Lastly, the "Others" category includes materials like ceramics and fabrics that have specific armor applications but are not as widely used as the aforementioned types.</p></p>
