<p><p>The Asphalt Release Agent market can be categorized into two types: liquid and solid. Liquid release agents are commonly used in the construction industry. They are in a liquid form and are sprayed onto the asphalt surface to prevent it from sticking to equipment and tools during the paving process. Solid release agents, on the other hand, come in the form of granules or powders that are spread onto the asphalt surface. They also serve the purpose of preventing the asphalt from sticking during construction activities. Both liquid and solid release agents play a critical role in ensuring smooth and efficient asphalt paving operations.</p></p>
