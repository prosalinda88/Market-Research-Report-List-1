<p><p>Automatic Meter Infrastructures (AMI) refer to a system of advanced smart meters that enable two-way communication between utility companies and consumers. Additionally, the rising adoption of renewable energy sources and smart home systems is further contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>One of the major trends in the AMI market is the deployment of advanced communication technologies like cellular networks and radio frequency mesh networks for seamless data transmission. This enables real-time monitoring and control of energy consumption, resulting in efficient load management and reduced wastage. Moreover, the integration of AMI with Internet of Things (IoT) technology enables the creation of intelligent energy management systems that optimize energy usage based on real-time data and user preferences.</p><p>Another notable trend is the emergence of smart cities and sustainable urban development initiatives.
