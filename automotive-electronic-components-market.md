<p><p>The automotive electronic components market can be categorized into two types: active components and passive components. Active components are electronic devices that require a power source to operate, such as microcontrollers, sensors, and transistors. These components actively control and manipulate electrical signals in the automotive system. On the other hand, passive components do not require power and include resistors, capacitors, inductors, and connectors. They primarily assist in storing, filtering, and regulating electrical signals in the automotive system. These two types of components play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of electronic systems in vehicles.</p></p>
