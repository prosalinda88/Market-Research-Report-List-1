<p><p>The automotive ignition coil market consists of two main types: single-spark and multi-spark. A single-spark ignition coil generates a single spark per ignition cycle, which ignites the air-fuel mixture in the engine's combustion chamber. On the other hand, a multi-spark ignition coil produces multiple sparks per ignition cycle, resulting in more efficient fuel combustion. The single-spark market caters to vehicles that require a basic ignition system, while the multi-spark market serves vehicles with higher performance demands, enhancing fuel efficiency and power output.</p></p>
