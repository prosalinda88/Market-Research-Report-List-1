The accelerator pedal is responsible for controlling the speed of the vehicle by regulating the flow of fuel to the engine. The brake pedal is used to slow down or stop the vehicle by applying pressure to the brake system. The clutch pedal is specifically found in manual transmission vehicles, allowing the driver to engage or disengage the engine from the transmission while changing gears. These pedals play crucial roles in ensuring safe and efficient vehicle operation.</p></p>
