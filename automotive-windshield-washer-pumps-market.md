<p><p>Automotive windshield washer pumps are available in two market types: mono pump and dual pump. Mono pump systems use a single pump to deliver washer fluid to both the front and rear windshields. On the other hand, dual pump systems consist of two separate pumps, one for each windshield. The dual pump setup allows for independent control and adjustment of the fluid flow for front and rear windshields. Both types of pumps are commonly used in vehicles to ensure effective and efficient cleaning of windshields.</p></p>
