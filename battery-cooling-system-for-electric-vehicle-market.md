<p><p>The battery cooling system for electric vehicles includes two main types: air cooling system and liquid cooling system. The air cooling system utilizes fans and blowers to dissipate heat from the battery pack, ensuring its optimal temperature. On the other hand, the liquid cooling system circulates a coolant through the battery pack, absorbing and transferring the heat away. Both systems aim to prevent overheating and maintain the battery's performance and lifespan. Each system has its advantages and disadvantages, and the choice typically depends on factors like cost, efficiency, and specific vehicle requirements.</p></p>
