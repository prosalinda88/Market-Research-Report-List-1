<p><p>Boat trailers are essential for transporting boats efficiently and securely. There are primarily two types of boat trailers: bunk trailers and roller trailers. Bunk trailers use a series of padded supports, or bunks, to cradle the boat during transportation. They offer excellent support and are ideal for heavier boats. On the other hand, roller trailers have a series of rollers that allow the boat to easily slide on and off the trailer. This type of trailer is ideal for lighter boats and makes launching and retrieving boats more accessible.</p></p>
