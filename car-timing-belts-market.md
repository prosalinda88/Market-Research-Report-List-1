<p><p>The car timing belts market is mainly divided into two types: trapezoidal tooth and arc tooth. Trapezoidal tooth timing belts are characterized by their trapezoidal-shaped teeth, which offer efficient power transmission and control. They are commonly used in engines and other industrial applications. On the other hand, arc tooth timing belts have a tooth profile with a radial arc, enabling smooth engagement and reduced noise. They are preferred in systems requiring high precision and low vibration. Both types serve distinct purposes within the car timing belts market, catering to different industrial needs.</p></p>
