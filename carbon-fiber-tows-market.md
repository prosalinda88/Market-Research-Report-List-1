<p><p>There are two main types of carbon fiber tows on the market: PAN based carbon fiber and pitch based carbon fiber. PAN based carbon fiber is produced from polyacrylonitrile fibers and is known for its high strength and modulus. It is commonly used in applications such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods. Pitch based carbon fiber, on the other hand, is derived from petroleum or coal tar pitch and has a higher thermal conductivity. It is often used in industries where heat resistance is crucial, like in the production of electronics and military equipment. Both types have their unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
