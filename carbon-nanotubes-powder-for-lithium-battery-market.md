<p><p>The carbon nanotubes powder used in lithium batteries are available in two market types: single-walled nanotubes (SWNTs) and multi-walled nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs are composed of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylinder, offering unique electrical and mechanical properties. Both types of nanotubes are utilized in lithium batteries to improve their energy storage capacity, efficiency, and overall performance.</p></p>
