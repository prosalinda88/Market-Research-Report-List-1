<p><p>The Chemical Metering Pump market consists of two main types: the Piston Chemical Metering Pump and the Hydraulic Chemical Metering Pump. A Piston Chemical Metering Pump utilizes reciprocating pistons to deliver accurate and precise amounts of chemicals. It is commonly used in various industries, including water treatment and oil refineries. On the other hand, a Hydraulic Chemical Metering Pump uses hydraulic power to inject chemicals. It is ideal for applications requiring high pressure and flow rates, often found in the petrochemical and chemical processing industries.</p></p>
