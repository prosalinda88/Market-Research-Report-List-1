<p><p>The children life vest market consists of three main types: foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests are made of buoyant foam material and provide constant flotation. Inflatable vests, on the other hand, feature an air bladder that can be inflated when needed, offering a compact design and easy movement. Hybrid vests combine the features of foam and inflatable vests, providing both buoyancy and additional air bladder support. Understanding the distinctions between these types is crucial for consumers when choosing the right life vest for their children's safety in water activities.</p></p>
