Photochromic materials change color when exposed to light, making them useful in sunglasses or smart windows. Thermochromic materials alter color with temperature variations, enabling applications in temperature indicators or toys. Electrochromic materials change color through electrical stimulation, commonly applied in smart glass windows or electronic displays.
