<p><p>The Civil Helicopter MRO market can be categorized into three main types: Airframe Heavy Maintenance, Engine Maintenance, and Component Maintenance. Airframe Heavy Maintenance involves the overall inspection, repair and upgrade of the main structure of the helicopter. Engine Maintenance focuses on the regular maintenance, repairing and replacement of the helicopter's engine system. Component Maintenance includes the repair, overhaul, and replacement of various components such as avionics, electrical systems, landing gear, and rotor systems.
