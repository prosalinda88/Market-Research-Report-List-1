<p><p>Cleaner dyes market includes three types of dyes - acid resistant, alkali resistant, and acid and alkali resistant dyes. Acid resistant dyes are specially designed to withstand acidic conditions, making them suitable for applications in acidic cleaning solutions. Alkali resistant dyes, on the other hand, can endure alkaline environments and are commonly used in alkaline cleaning agents. Acid and alkali resistant dyes possess the ability to resist both acidic and alkaline conditions, making them versatile and ideal for various cleaning solutions that may have varying pH levels.</p></p>
