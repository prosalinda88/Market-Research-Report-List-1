<p><p>The coconut shell activated carbon market can be classified into three types: powdered activated carbon, granular activated carbon, and others. Powdered activated carbon is a finely ground form that offers a high surface area for adsorption and is commonly used in water treatment and air purification. Granular activated carbon is a larger particle size, providing a slower adsorption rate but longer-lasting effects. It is widely used in various industries, including wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals. The "others" category encompasses specialized forms of coconut shell activated carbon, such as extruded pellets or impregnated activated carbon.</p></p>
