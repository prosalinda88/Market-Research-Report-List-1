<p><p>The colored glass market encompasses two main types: plate glass and special glass. Plate glass refers to flat glass sheets that are widely used in construction and automotive industries. It is produced by pouring molten glass onto a bed of molten tin, resulting in a flat and even surface. On the other hand, special glass refers to glass with unique characteristics such as heat resistance, UV resistance, or enhanced strength. Special glass finds applications in various industries such as electronics, telecommunications, and medical devices.</p></p>
