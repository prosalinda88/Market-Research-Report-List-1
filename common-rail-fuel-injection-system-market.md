<p><p>The Common Rail Fuel Injection System is available in two types: Solenoid type and Piezo type. The solenoid type market refers to a fuel injection system that uses electromagnetic coils to control the fuel injectors. It is widely used in commercial vehicles due to its cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, the piezo type market consists of a fuel injection system that utilizes piezoelectric crystals to control fuel injectors. It offers greater precision and faster response times, making it suitable for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
