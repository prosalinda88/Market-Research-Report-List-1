<p><p>Conductive FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers) are specialized packaging solutions used to store and transport static-sensitive materials. They come in two main types: woven and non-woven. Woven conductive FIBC are made by weaving conductive threads into a fabric, providing strength and durability. Non-woven conductive FIBC, on the other hand, are created by bonding fibers together using heat or chemicals. Both types offer excellent electrostatic discharge properties, preventing the buildup of static electricity and protecting the contents from potential damage during handling, storage, and transportation.</p></p>
