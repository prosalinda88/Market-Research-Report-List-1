<p><p>Continuous vacuum filters are commonly used in various industries for solid-liquid separation processes. There are several types of continuous vacuum filters available in the market. Disc filters use a series of rotating discs to capture and separate solids from liquids. Drum filters operate by rotating a cylindrical drum coated with a filter medium to remove solids. Drum pre-coat filters involve the addition of a pre-coat layer on the drum to improve filtration efficiency. Additionally, there are other variations of continuous vacuum filters designed to meet specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
