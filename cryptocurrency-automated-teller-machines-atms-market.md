<p><p>Cryptocurrency Automated Teller Machines (ATMs) come in two main market types: 1-way Model and 2-way Model. In a 1-way Model, users can only buy cryptocurrencies using cash, and cannot sell them. These ATMs serve as a convenient way for individuals to purchase cryptocurrencies using fiat currency. On the other hand, a 2-way Model allows users to both buy and sell cryptocurrencies. These ATMs provide a complete solution for users to convert between cryptocurrencies and fiat currency, offering greater flexibility and convenience.</p></p>
