<p><p>The denim fabric market can be classified into three types based on weight: lightweight denim, medium denim, and heavy denim. Lightweight denim refers to denim fabrics that are thin and have a lower weight per square yard, making them suitable for warmer climates and light clothing items like shirts. Medium denim falls in the middle, offering a moderate weight and thickness, making it versatile for various clothing types. Heavy denim, on the other hand, is thicker and heavier, usually used for durable garments like jeans and jackets that require more strength and durability.</p></p>
