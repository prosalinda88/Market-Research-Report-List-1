<p><p>Digital Marketing Software (DMS) comes in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based DMS refers to software that is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs as it does not require installation and can be accessed from any location. On the other hand, on-premise DMS is installed locally on the user's servers and infrastructure. This type provides greater control and security but usually involves higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of businesses in their digital marketing efforts.</p></p>
