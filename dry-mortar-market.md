<p><p>Dry mortar is a building material used for binding bricks, stones, and other construction materials together to form a solid structure. Rising urbanization, rapid industrialization, and the growing demand for affordable housing are the key factors driving the market growth. Additionally, the demand for dry mortar is also fueled by its advantages such as improved workability, reduced curing time, and lower maintenance costs.</p><p>The market analysis indicates that the Asia-Pacific region dominates the dry mortar market due to the rapid growth in the construction sector in countries like China and India.
