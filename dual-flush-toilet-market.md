<p><p>The Dual Flush Toilet market consists of three types: Ball Valve Toilet, Self-controlled Water-saving Toilet, and Pneumatic Toilet. Ball Valve Toilet utilizes a simple mechanism with a ball valve to control water flow during flushing, enabling two flushing options. Self-controlled Water-saving Toilet incorporates advanced technology that automatically adjusts flushing power and water usage based on need. Pneumatic Toilet uses air pressure to regulate flushing, offering efficient water usage and quieter operation. These types cater to different customer preferences, providing options for water conservation and efficient flushing.</p></p>
