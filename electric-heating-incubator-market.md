<p><p>The electric heating incubator market comprises two main types: constant temperature incubators and variable temperature incubators. Constant temperature incubators maintain a steady temperature throughout the incubation process, ensuring consistent and predictable results. Variable temperature incubators, on the other hand, allow users to adjust the temperature according to specific requirements. Both types offer controlled environments for the growth of organisms, but constant temperature incubators focus on stability, while variable temperature incubators offer flexibility in temperature settings.</p></p>
