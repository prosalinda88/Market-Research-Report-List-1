<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Communication Controller (EVCC) market can be categorized into two types: Wired and Wireless. Wired EVCC involves the use of physical cables or wires to establish communication between electric vehicles and charging infrastructure. This type of communication offers a reliable and secure connection, ensuring efficient data transfer and power transmission. On the other hand, Wireless EVCC utilizes technologies such as radio frequency to establish communication without the need for physical connections. While wireless communication provides convenience and flexibility, it may slightly compromise on data transfer speed and security compared to wired communication.</p></p>
