<p><p>The Electronic Grade Dimethyl Carbonate (DMC) market can be categorized into two types: Propylene Oxide Method and Ethylene Oxide Method. The Propylene Oxide Method involves the reaction of propylene oxide with CO2 and methanol to produce DMC. This method offers advantages such as high DMC yield and low environmental impact. On the other hand, the Ethylene Oxide Method utilizes ethylene oxide instead of propylene oxide. Both methods are commonly used in the production of Electronic Grade DMC, which is widely utilized in the electronics industry for applications such as electrolytes in lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
