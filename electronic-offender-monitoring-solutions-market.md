<p><p>Electronic Offender Monitoring Solutions are used to track and monitor individuals who are under house arrest or probation. There are two main types of monitoring systems: Radio Frequency (RF) and Global Positioning System (GPS). RF systems use radio signals and a monitoring device to create a virtual boundary around the offender's residence. If the offender moves outside this boundary, an alert is triggered. GPS systems track the offender's location in real-time using satellite technology, providing continuous monitoring and alerts if the person enters a prohibited area.</p></p>
