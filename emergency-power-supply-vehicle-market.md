<p><p>Emergency Power Supply Vehicles are specialized vehicles designed to provide power backup and support during emergency situations. They come in two main types: Box Type and Trailer Type. </p><p>Box Type vehicles are compact and self-contained, with an integrated power generator and storage space for fuel or batteries. They can be easily transported to different locations and provide immediate power supply in disaster-stricken areas or remote locations.</p><p>Trailer Type vehicles, on the other hand, consist of a power generator mounted on a trailer. They offer larger power capacity and are suitable for longer durations of power supply. These vehicles can be easily hitched to another vehicle for transportation and serve as mobile power stations in emergency situations.</p></p>
