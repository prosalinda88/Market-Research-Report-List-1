<p><p>Engineering adhesives are crucial components in various industries as they offer efficient bonding solutions. Reactive acrylic adhesive, such as SGA or UV adhesive, is commonly used due to its fast curing and high strength properties. Anaerobic adhesives are ideal for applications where the absence of air is desired for curing. Cyanoacrylate adhesive provides fast and strong bonding for various materials. Epoxy adhesive offers excellent resistance and durability. Silicone adhesive provides flexibility, heat resistance, and electrical insulation. Solvent-free reactive PU adhesives are widely used for their high strength and long-lasting bonding capabilities.</p></p>
