<p><p>EVA resins and films are types of polymers commonly used in various industries. EVA resins refer to the raw material from which EVA films are made. These resins are highly versatile and possess excellent adhesion, flexibility, and low-temperature toughness properties. EVA films, on the other hand, are thin sheets or membranes produced by melting and cooling the EVA resins. These films find applications in packaging, solar panels, laminated glass, and other industries due to their durability, transparency, and resistance to chemicals and UV radiation.</p></p>
