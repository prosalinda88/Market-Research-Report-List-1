Nylon FDY Yarns are continuous filaments made from nylon polymers. They offer excellent abrasion resistance, high strength, and good elasticity. The "Others" category includes different types of FDY yarns, such as viscose FDY yarns, spandex FDY yarns, and silk FDY yarns, each offering unique properties suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
