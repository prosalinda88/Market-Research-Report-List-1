<p><p>Flakes Recycled Thermoplastics Market Types can be categorized as Non-Biodegradable and Biodegradable. Non-Biodegradable thermoplastics, such as polyethylene terephthalate (PET) or polypropylene (PP), do not decompose naturally. They offer long-term durability and recyclability, making them suitable for applications where sustainability is important. On the other hand, Biodegradable thermoplastics, like polylactic acid (PLA) or polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA), can be broken down by microorganisms, reducing their environmental impact. Biodegradable thermoplastics are ideal for single-use products or applications where compostability is desired, but they may have limitations in terms of performance and cost.</p></p>
