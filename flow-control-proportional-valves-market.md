They are primarily used in applications where precise control and accuracy are crucial, such as industrial automation and robotics. On the other hand, Semi Automatic valves require some level of human intervention to regulate the fluid flow. These valves are commonly used in applications where manual adjustments or monitoring are necessary, such as agricultural irrigation systems or some manufacturing processes.</p></p>
