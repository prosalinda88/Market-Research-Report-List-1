<p><p>The fluorescent fiber optic market comprises two types: Polystyrene type and PMMA type. Polystyrene type fluorescent fiber optics are made using polystyrene as the core material, which exhibits good light transmission properties. They are commonly used in applications like communication equipment and decorative lighting. On the other hand, PMMA type fluorescent fiber optics are made using polymethyl methacrylate as the core material. They have excellent flexibility and are widely utilized in areas such as automotive lighting and biomedical devices due to their superior light guiding capabilities.</p></p>
