<p><p>Formaldehyde scavengers market involves different types, namely neutralization reaction, catalytic decomposition, oxidation-reduction, and sealing plugging. Catalytic decomposition employs a catalyst to break down formaldehyde into non-toxic components. Oxidation-reduction refers to the reaction where formaldehyde is oxidized or reduced to form harmless substances. Lastly, sealing plugging involves physically sealing off and preventing the release of formaldehyde. These methods aim to reduce or eliminate the harmful effects of formaldehyde in various industries.</p></p>
