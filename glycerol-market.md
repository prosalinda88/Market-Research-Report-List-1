<p><p>Glycerol, also known as glycerin or glycerine, is a colorless and odorless liquid that is commonly used in various industries. It is also used in the production of tobacco, where it acts as a humectant to prevent the tobacco from drying out. In the food and beverage industry, it is used as a sweetener and preservative. In the cosmetics industry, glycerol is used as a moisturizer in various skin care and hair care products.</p><p>The glycerol market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years.
