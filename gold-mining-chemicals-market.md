<p><p>The gold mining chemicals market can be categorized into two main types: sodium cyanide and emulsifiers. Sodium cyanide is a commonly used chemical in gold extraction processes, as it helps to separate gold from its ore. It acts as a leaching and refining agent. Emulsifiers, on the other hand, are used to improve the efficiency of gold mining processes by reducing interfacial tension between oil and water phases. They aid in the separation and extraction of gold particles, enhancing overall productivity in the industry.</p></p>
