<p><p>Green roof systems refer to the types of roofs that are covered with vegetation, providing numerous environmental benefits. Layered green roof systems consist of multiple layers, including a waterproofing layer, drainage layer, growing medium, and vegetation layer, offering a traditional approach to green roofs. On the other hand, modular green roof systems involve pre-grown plants or vegetation installed in modules or trays, allowing for flexibility and easy installation. Both types of green roof systems contribute to enhanced energy efficiency, stormwater management, and aesthetic value to buildings.</p></p>
