<p><p>The handheld gamma detector market includes various types of detectors such as CsI, NaI, LaBr, Geiger Muller, and Others. CsI detectors consist of cesium iodide crystals, offering good energy resolution and efficiency. NaI detectors contain sodium iodide crystals, providing high sensitivity for gamma radiation. LaBr detectors are composed of lanthanum bromide crystals, ensuring excellent energy resolution and high counting rates. Geiger Muller detectors use a Geiger-Muller tube, suitable for detecting low levels of gamma radiation. Other types encompass different technologies or materials used for gamma detection, catering to specific application requirements.</p></p>
