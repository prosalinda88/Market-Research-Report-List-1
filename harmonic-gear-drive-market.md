<p><p>Harmonic gear drive, also known as strain wave gear or harmonic drive, is a type of gear mechanism commonly used in industrial applications. <p><p>The Harmonic Gear Drive market can be categorized into three main types: Cup Style, Hat Style, and Pancake Style. Cup Style harmonic gear drives consist of a flexible cup-shaped outer spline, a rigid circular spline, and a wave generator that creates the motion. Hat Style harmonic gear drives have a hat-shaped flexible spline, a circular spline, and a wave generator. Pancake Style harmonic gear drives have a thin disc-shaped spline, a circular spline, and a wave generator. These different types offer variations in design and usage for various applications within the harmonic gear drive market.</p></p>
