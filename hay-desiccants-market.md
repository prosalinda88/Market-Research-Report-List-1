<p><p>Hay desiccants are used to accelerate the drying process of hay in order to prevent mold growth and maintain its nutritional value. There are different types of hay desiccants available in the market. Sulfur-based desiccants, which release sulfur dioxide upon contact with moisture, are effective in preventing mold formation. Buffered propionic acid desiccants work by inhibiting the growth of molds and yeasts. Other types of hay desiccants include those that contain organic acids or have a combination of ingredients that prevent moisture retention and mold formation.</p></p>
