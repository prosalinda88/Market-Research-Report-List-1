<p><p>The hazardous waste handling automation market offers various types of equipment for efficient waste management. Manipulator arms are robotic arms designed to handle and manipulate hazardous materials. Telescoping masts are vertical structures with adjustable height, used to transport waste containers. Cranes are lifting machines used for transferring heavy waste materials. Trusses are frameworks used for supporting equipment during waste disposal processes. Size reduction systems are machines that crush or shred waste into smaller pieces, facilitating its disposal. These technologies play a crucial role in automating and streamlining hazardous waste handling processes.</p></p>
