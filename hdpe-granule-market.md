<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>HDPE Granule market types include the Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. The Autoclave Process involves using a high-pressure reactor to produce HDPE granules by polymerizing ethylene gas with a catalyst. This process is suitable for producing a wide range of HDPE granules with various properties. On the other hand, the Tubular Process involves extruding the molten HDPE through a tubular reactor to form granules. This process offers better control over the properties of the HDPE granules and is commonly used for producing highly specialized and high-quality products.</p></p>
