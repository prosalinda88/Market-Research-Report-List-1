<p><p>Heat-shrinkable tubing is available in various types. Polyolefin heat-shrinkable tubing is the most common type and offers excellent insulation and durability. Fluoropolymer heat-shrinkable tubing provides superior chemical resistance and can withstand high temperatures. Chlorinated polyolefin heat-shrinkable tubing is flame retardant and ideal for automotive and military applications. Other types include PVC heat-shrinkable tubing, which is cost-effective, and silicone heat-shrinkable tubing, which offers flexibility and high performance in extreme conditions. Each type caters to different needs, providing unique properties and benefits for various industries and applications.</p></p>
