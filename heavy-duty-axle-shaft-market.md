<p><p>The heavy-duty axle shaft market consists of various types, such as propeller shafts and half shafts. Propeller shafts are used in vehicles with a rear-wheel or all-wheel drive, enabling power transmission from the transmission to the rear differential. On the other hand, half shafts are utilized in front-wheel drive vehicles, transferring power from the transmission to the front wheels. Both these types of axle shafts play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient power transmission, enhancing the performance and overall durability of heavy-duty vehicles.</p></p>
