<p><p>High-pressure long tube trailers are used to transport compressed gases such as hydrogen or helium. There are two main market types for these trailers: frame type and bundled type. </p><p>Frame type trailers have multiple individual cylinders or tubes that are secured within a frame structure. These trailers are typically used for transporting smaller quantities of gases and provide easy access for loading and unloading.</p><p>Bundled type trailers consist of long tubes bundled together into a single unit. This design allows for the transportation of larger quantities of gases and provides increased safety and stability during transit.</p></p>
