<p><p>Home non-invasive ventilators for COPD come in two main types: single level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single level ventilators deliver a consistent pressure throughout the respiratory cycle, helping maintain lung function and improve breathing for COPD patients. On the other hand, bilevel ventilators offer two different pressures, one during inhalation and another during exhalation, providing support for individuals who have difficulty exhaling effectively. These ventilators are designed for use in home settings, allowing COPD patients to receive the necessary respiratory support without needing to be hospitalized.</p></p>
