<p><p>The HVAC insulation market consists of different types of products such as wraps, tapes, adhesives, and sealants. Wraps are materials used to cover and insulate HVAC pipes and ducts, preventing heat loss or gain. Tapes are used to secure and seal insulation materials, ensuring a proper installation. Adhesives are used to bond insulation materials together, creating a tight and effective insulation system. Sealants, on the other hand, are used to fill gaps and cracks in insulation, preventing air leakage and improving energy efficiency.</p></p>
