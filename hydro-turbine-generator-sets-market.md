<p><p>Hydro turbine generator sets are used to generate electricity from the flow of water. There are several types of hydro turbine generator sets available in the market. The Francis turbine is the most commonly used type, suitable for medium to high head applications. The Kaplan turbine is ideal for low to medium head applications and can adjust its blades based on the flow of water. The diagonal turbine is used for low to medium head applications, and the tubular turbine is suitable for medium to high head applications. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the water flow characteristics.</p></p>

<p><p>The hydro turbine generator sets market refers to the industry that deals with the manufacturing and selling of turbine generator sets for hydroelectric power generation. Impulse turbines are a type of hydro turbine that convert the kinetic energy of a high-velocity water jet into mechanical energy. They are suitable for high head and low discharge applications. On the other hand, reaction turbines convert the pressure energy of water passing through the runner blades into mechanical energy. They are used in low head and high discharge applications. Both types of turbines play a crucial role in the hydro energy sector.</p></p>
