<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Ethernet Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Managed</li><li>Lightly Managed</li><li>Unmanaged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Ethernet switches are classified into three types based on their manageability - managed, lightly managed, and unmanaged. </p><p>Managed switches provide advanced features like VLAN configuration, Quality of Service management, and security protocols. They offer greater control and customization options for industrial networks.</p><p>Lightly managed switches have limited management capabilities. They provide basic features like port monitoring and simple network configuration, but lack the advanced functionality of managed switches.</p><p>Unmanaged switches are the simplest and most cost-effective option. They lack any management features and are typically plug-and-play devices, suitable for small-scale industrial networks with basic connectivity needs.</p></p>
