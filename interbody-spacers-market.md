Titanium spacers, on the other hand, are made of a biocompatible metal that offers excellent mechanical properties and durability. Both PEEK and titanium interbody spacers are commonly utilized in the treatment of various spinal conditions, providing surgeons with options to choose from based on patient characteristics and surgeon preference.</p></p>
