Air-cooled machines use a built-in fan to dissipate heat generated during the plasma cutting process, making them more compact and suitable for lighter applications. On the other hand, water-cooled machines use a water cooling system to regulate the temperature, allowing for continuous and heavy-duty cutting operations.
