<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Hot Melt-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The label adhesives market is categorized into three types: water-based, hot melt-based, and solvent-based. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and offer good adhesion to various substrates. Hot melt-based adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, providing fast adhesion and versatility. Solvent-based adhesives use solvents to achieve adhesion and are known for their high initial tack and long-term durability. Each type has its own unique features and advantages, catering to different application requirements in industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
