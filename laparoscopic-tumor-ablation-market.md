<p><p>Laparoscopic tumor ablation is a minimally invasive surgical procedure that aims to remove tumors or cancerous tissues from various organs within the body. This procedure involves the use of laparoscopic surgical instruments, including a thin tube with a camera and surgical tools, which are inserted through small incisions in the abdomen. The surgeon uses these instruments to heat or freeze the tumor, causing it to shrink and eventually die off. Laparoscopic tumor ablation offers several advantages over traditional open surgery, including smaller incisions, reduced postoperative pain, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery times.</p><p>The Laparoscopic Tumor Ablation Market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving this market growth is the rising incidence of cancer worldwide.
