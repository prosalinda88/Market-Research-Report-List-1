<p><p>The latex boxed glove market consists of two main types: non-sterile and sterile gloves. Non-sterile gloves are typically used in situations where a high level of sanitation is not required, such as general cleaning or food handling. These gloves are not individually wrapped and may not offer the same level of protection against pathogens. On the other hand, sterile gloves are individually wrapped and are essential in medical and surgical settings to prevent contamination and the spread of infections. Sterile gloves undergo a higher level of quality control to ensure their effectiveness in maintaining a sterile environment.</p></p>
