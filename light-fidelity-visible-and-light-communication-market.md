<p><p>Light Fidelity Visible and Light Communication (Li-Fi) is a wireless communication technology that uses visible light as a medium to transmit data. It can enhance indoor navigation, smart lighting systems, improve connectivity in highly populated areas, and provide secure data transmission in sensitive environments. Photoresistors receive the light signals and convert them back into electrical signals for data extraction. MCUs coordinate the transmission and reception process, allowing efficient communication.
