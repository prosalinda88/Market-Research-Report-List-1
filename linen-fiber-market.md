<p><p>The linen fiber market is categorized into two types: long flax fiber and short flax fiber. Long flax fiber refers to the high-quality linen fibers extracted from the inner part of the flax plant stem. It possesses excellent strength and longer length, making it ideal for applications like high-end textiles and fabrics. On the other hand, short flax fiber, obtained from the outer layer of the flax plant stem, has slightly lower strength and shorter length. It is mainly utilized in coarser materials like twine, ropes, and paper products.</p></p>
