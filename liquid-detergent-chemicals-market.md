<p><p>The liquid detergent chemicals market is categorized into different types based on the composition of the detergents. Anionic detergents are negatively charged molecules that are effective in removing dirt and stains. Cationic detergents are positively charged and are commonly used as fabric softeners. Non-ionic detergents do not have an electrical charge and are known for their mildness on fabrics. Lastly, zwitterionic detergents have both positive and negative charges, making them effective at cleaning and preventing the buildup of soap scum.</p></p>
