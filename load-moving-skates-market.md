<p><p>Load moving skates are specialized equipment used for moving heavy loads in various industries. The market for load moving skates can be categorized into four main types: fixed wheel load moving skates, steerable machinery skates, swivel castor load moving skates, and other types. </p><p>Fixed wheel load moving skates have wheels that can only move in a straight line, making them suitable for linear movements. Steerable machinery skates, on the other hand, have wheels that can be turned, allowing for easy maneuverability in different directions. Swivel castor load moving skates have rotating wheels, enabling smooth movement in any direction. Lastly, the "other" market includes load moving skates with unique features or specialized applications that do not fall under the previous categories.</p></p>
