<p><p>The low voltage power cable market can be grouped into two types: ground cable and underground cable. Ground cables are designed to be installed on the surface of the ground, typically used for temporary power supply in construction sites or outdoor events. On the other hand, underground cables are specifically designed for being buried underground, providing a reliable long-term power distribution solution for urban areas or rural regions. Both types of cables serve the purpose of transmitting low voltage power, but they differ in their installation methods and applications.</p></p>
