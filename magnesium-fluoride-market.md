<p><p>Magnesium fluoride is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula MgF2. It is a white crystalline solid and is commonly used in various industries. This compound is known for its high melting point and good thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in optical and electrical industries.</p><p>In the optical industry, magnesium fluoride is used as a coating material for lenses, windows, and mirrors due to its low refractive index and high transmittance for ultraviolet, visible, and infrared light. It is also utilized in the production of ceramics, catalysts, and other materials. In the electrical industry, magnesium fluoride is used as an insulating material for high voltage power transmission systems.</p><p>The current outlook of the magnesium fluoride market is positive, and it is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years.
