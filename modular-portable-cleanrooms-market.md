<p><p>Modular Portable Cleanrooms are categorized into two main types: Softwall Cleanrooms and Hardwall Cleanrooms. Softwall Cleanrooms are flexible and lightweight structures that use curtains or panels made of materials such as vinyl or PVC to create a controlled environment. They are easy to assemble, disassemble, and relocate. On the other hand, Hardwall Cleanrooms are sturdier and more permanent structures built with solid walls made of materials like aluminum or stainless steel. They provide enhanced durability and a higher level of control over the environment by minimizing air leakage and offering higher cleanliness standards.</p></p>
