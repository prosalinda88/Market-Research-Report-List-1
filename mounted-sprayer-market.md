<p><strong>The Mounted Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-point Hitch</li><li>ATV-mounted</li><li>Front-mount</li><li>Straddle Tractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mounted sprayer market includes three prominent types: 3-point hitch, ATV-mounted, and front-mount. The 3-point hitch sprayers are designed to be attached at the rear of tractors, utilizing a hydraulic mechanism for controlling the sprayer's movement. ATV-mounted sprayers, as the name suggests, are specifically designed to be mounted on all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), enhancing their mobility and flexibility. Front-mount sprayers are attached to the front part of tractors, providing better visibility and maneuverability. Lastly, the straddle tractor market refers to sprayers that are mounted on specialized straddle tractors, offering improved stability and control during spraying operations.</p></p>
