<p><p>The natural gum market can be divided into two main types: food grade and industry grade. Food-grade gums are those that are safe for human consumption and are commonly used as thickeners, stabilizers, and emulsifiers in various food and beverage products. Industry-grade gums, on the other hand, are primarily used in non-food applications such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and textiles. These gums may have different compositions or purities compared to food-grade gums, but they serve similar purposes in providing viscosity, adhesive, or binding properties.</p></p>
