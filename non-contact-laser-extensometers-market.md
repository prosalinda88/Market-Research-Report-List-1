Built-in laser extensometers are integrated into the material testing machine and provide accurate measurements during the testing process. Frame laser extensometers, on the other hand, are external devices attached to the testing frame and provide measurements for a wide range of testing machines. Both types of laser extensometers offer reliable and precise strain measurement capabilities.</p></p>
