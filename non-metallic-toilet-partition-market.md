<p><p>The non-metallic toilet partition market primarily consists of three types: plastic laminate, phenolic resin, and others. Plastic laminate partitions are made by bonding layers of resin-impregnated kraft paper together, resulting in a durable and affordable option. Phenolic resin partitions, on the other hand, are composed of multiple layers of resin-impregnated cellulose fibers, providing exceptional strength and resistance to moisture, impact, and vandalism. The "others" category encompasses a variety of materials such as solid surface, high-density polyethylene (HDPE), and compact laminate, each offering unique characteristics and benefits to cater to specific needs.</p></p>
