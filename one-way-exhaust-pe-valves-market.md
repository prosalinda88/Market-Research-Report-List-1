<p><p>One-way exhaust PE valves are used in various markets and come in three different types: round, square, and S shape. Round valves have a circular opening and are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications where space is limited. Square valves have a square-shaped opening that allows for more volume flow and are often used in ventilation systems. S shape valves feature a unique design that reduces noise and turbulence, making them suitable for applications where silence and efficiency are crucial, such as HVAC systems.</p></p>
