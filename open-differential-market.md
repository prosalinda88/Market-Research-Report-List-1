<p><p>The open differential market can be classified into two main types: conical planetary gear differential and cylindrical planetary gear differential. Conical planetary gear differentials are characterized by their cone-shaped gears, which allow for better torque distribution and increased stability. On the other hand, cylindrical planetary gear differentials feature cylindrical gears, providing smoother power transmission and improved efficiency. Both types offer unique advantages and are commonly used in various vehicles to optimize traction and maneuverability.</p></p>
