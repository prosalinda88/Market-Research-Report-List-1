<p><p>The optical coating market includes various types of coatings that are used to enhance the performance of optical components. AR coatings (anti-reflective) are designed to minimize reflection and increase light transmission. High reflective coatings enhance the reflectivity of optical components. Transparent conductive coatings provide conductivity while maintaining transparency. Filter coatings are used to selectively transmit or reflect specific wavelengths of light. Beamsplitter coatings divide incident light into two or more beams. Ec coatings (environmentally friendly) are designed to be more sustainable and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
