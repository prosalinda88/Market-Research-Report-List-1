<p><p>Optical Grade PET Film is classified into various types based on its applications. Reflective Film is used to enhance visibility and reflectivity in different applications like signage, lighting, and displays. AR Film, or Anti-Reflective Film, minimizes reflection and glare on surfaces like electronic screens, eyeglasses, and camera lenses. Filter Film is designed to block specific wavelengths or colors and is commonly used in LCD displays and optical filters. Lastly, the Others category includes PET films that have unique properties or applications, such as thermal films or conductive films.</p></p>
