<p><p>The Optically Clear Adhesive Tape Market can be categorized into two main types: liquid-based and non-liquid tape. Liquid-based tapes are made by mixing adhesive substances with a solvent to form a liquid solution, which is then applied and solidified onto a surface. Non-liquid tapes, on the other hand, are pre-formed adhesive tapes that do not require any mixing or solvent. Both types of tapes serve the same purpose of providing optical clarity, enabling them to be used in various applications such as electronic devices, displays, and automotive industries.</p></p>
