<p><p>Pad printing ink market is divided into several types. One-component ink is a ready-to-use ink that does not require any additional components. Two-component ink, on the other hand, needs a catalyst or hardener for curing. Dry ink is solvent-based and dries quickly. Oxidation ink undergoes a chemical reaction with air to dry. Sublimation ink transforms from a solid to gas state when heated, leaving a print on the desired surface. Special inks include UV-curable, fluorescent, and metallic inks, catering to specific printing requirements.</p></p>
