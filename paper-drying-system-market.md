<p><p>The paper drying system market offers various types of drying systems to meet the specific needs of paper manufacturers. Rolling bed dryers involve passing the paper over slowly moving heated rolls, effectively removing moisture. Air dryers utilize a stream of hot air to evaporate moisture from the paper. Convection dryers circulate heated air around the paper to facilitate drying. Conduction dryers involve direct contact with heated surfaces to remove moisture. Other types may include vacuum dryers, infrared dryers, and freeze dryers, each utilizing different methods to dry paper efficiently.</p></p>
