<p><p>The market for paper pigments can be categorized into three types: calcium carbonate, kaolin, and others. Calcium carbonate is a widely used pigment in the paper industry due to its brightness and opacity. Kaolin, on the other hand, is a clay mineral that offers excellent print gloss and ink absorbency. The "others" category includes various pigments like titanium dioxide, talc, and synthetic pigments. These pigments provide specific characteristics such as improved whiteness, color, and smoothness to the paper, enhancing its printability and visual appeal.</p></p>
