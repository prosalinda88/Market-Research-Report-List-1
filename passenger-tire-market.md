<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of passenger tire markets: radial tire and bias tire. Radial tires have a construction where the layers of cords are arranged perpendicularly to the direction of the tire's rotation, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. On the other hand, bias tires have a construction where the cords are laid diagonally across the tire, providing greater load-carrying capacity and durability. Both types of tires have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
