<p><p>The passenger vehicle gasoline engine exhaust valve market can be divided into three types: mono metallic valve, bimetallic valve, and hollow valve. Mono metallic valves are made of a single material and are cost-effective. Bimetallic valves are composed of two different materials, offering better durability and resistance to heat and wear. Hollow valves have a hollow core, making them lighter and more efficient in heat dissipation. Each type has its own advantages and suitability for different engine requirements, offering options for manufacturers and consumers in the market.</p></p>
