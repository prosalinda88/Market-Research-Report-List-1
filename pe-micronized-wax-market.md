There are two main types of PE Micronized Wax in the market: Polyethylene Waxes and Oxidized Polyethylene Waxes. Polyethylene waxes are made from the polymerization of ethylene and have a low molecular weight. They are commonly used in various industries such as coatings, inks, and adhesives. Oxidized polyethylene waxes, on the other hand, are chemically modified with an oxidizing agent. This modification increases their compatibility with polar materials and enhances their dispersibility in water-based systems. Both types of waxes have unique properties that make them suitable for different applications.</p></p>
