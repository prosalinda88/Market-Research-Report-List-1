<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photosensitive Reversible</li><li>Photosensitive Irreversible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive polymer market consists of two types: photosensitive reversible and photosensitive irreversible. Photosensitive reversible polymers undergo changes in their properties when exposed to light, but return to their original state when the light source is removed. These polymers are commonly used in optical applications, such as light-sensitive coatings. On the other hand, photosensitive irreversible polymers experience permanent changes in their properties upon exposure to light, making them suitable for applications such as photoresists in the semiconductor industry. Both types cater to different needs and have their own unique set of applications in various industries.</p></p>
