<p><p>Polysulfone products refer to a type of high-performance engineering plastic that offers excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical strength. This market is comprised of three main types: PSU (Polysulfone), PES (Polyethersulfone), and PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone). PSU is known for its transparency, rigidity, and resistance to high temperatures. PES is favored for its high-temperature resistance and dimensional stability. PPSU stands out for its exceptional impact strength, hydrolytic stability, and resistance to sterilization methods. These products find applications in various industries like medical, automotive, aerospace, and electronics due to their unique properties.</p></p>
