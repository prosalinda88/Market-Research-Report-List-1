<p><p>Polyurea-based coatings are a type of protective coating that offer excellent durability, corrosion resistance, and chemical resistance. They are commonly categorized into two main types: aromatic based and aliphatic based. Aromatic-based polyurea coatings are more cost-effective and have good UV resistance, but tend to yellow over time when exposed to sunlight. On the other hand, aliphatic-based polyurea coatings are more expensive but provide superior color stability and UV resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications where aesthetics are important.</p></p>
