<p><p>The porous metal market can be categorized into three types based on porosity levels. Low porosity, below 30%, refers to metals with tightly packed and minimal pore space. Medium porosity, ranging from 30% to 60%, represents metals that offer a moderate level of pore space. High porosity, above 60%, indicates metals with a significant amount of interconnected pores. These different types of porous metals cater to diverse applications, with lower porosity metals suitable for filtration, medium porosity metals ideal for heat transfer, and higher porosity metals effective for lightweight structural components.</p></p>
