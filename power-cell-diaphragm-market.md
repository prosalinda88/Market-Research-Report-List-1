<p><p>The power cell diaphragm market encompasses three main types: wet diaphragm, dry single pull diaphragm, and dry double pull diaphragm. Wet diaphragms are filled with a conductive electrolyte, allowing for ion flow and electrochemical reactions. Dry single pull diaphragms have a mesoporous separator that prevents direct contact between the electrodes. Dry double pull diaphragms have two separate seals, enhancing safety and reducing the risk of leakage. These variations cater to diverse application needs and offer improved efficiency, reliability, and safety in power cell technologies.</p></p>
