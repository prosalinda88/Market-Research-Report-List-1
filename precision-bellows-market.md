The increasing demand for precision engineering in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, electronics, and healthcare is driving the market growth. Precision bellows are used in these industries for applications such as controlling fluid flow, compensating for dimensional changes, and absorbing vibration and shock.</p><p>Additionally, the growing focus on safety and reliability in the automotive and aerospace sectors is further fueling the demand for precision bellows.
