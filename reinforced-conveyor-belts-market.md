<p><p>Reinforced conveyor belts are categorized into two main types based on their weight: heavy weight conveyer belts and light weight conveyer belts. Heavy weight conveyer belts are designed to handle large and heavy materials, such as bulk solids, coal, minerals, and metals. They are characterized by their durability and high tensile strength. On the other hand, light weight conveyer belts are designed for smaller and lighter materials, such as packaged goods and small parts. They are known for their flexibility and ease of transportation.</p></p>
