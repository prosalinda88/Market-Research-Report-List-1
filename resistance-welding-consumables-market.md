<p><p>Resistance welding consumables include stick electrodes, solid wires, flux cored wires, SAW (submerged arc welding) wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are commonly used in manual and robotic resistance welding applications. Solid wires are versatile and suitable for different materials, while flux cored wires have a flux-filled center that provides protection and enhances weld quality. SAW wires are used in applications that require high deposition rates and deep weld penetration. Fluxes are used to remove impurities, prevent oxidation, and improve the overall weld quality.</p></p>
