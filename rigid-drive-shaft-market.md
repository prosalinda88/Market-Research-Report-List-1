<p><p>A rigid drive shaft, also known as a propeller shaft or cardan shaft, is a mechanical component used in automotive, industrial, and marine applications to transmit torque from the engine or motor to the driven wheels or propellers. It consists of a long rod with universal joints at both ends, allowing it to bend and rotate, thus compensating for the misalignment between the engine and the driven component.</p><p>The future outlook for the rigid drive shaft market is promising. The increasing demand for vehicles, especially commercial vehicles, coupled with the growing industrial and marine sectors, is expected to drive the market's growth.
