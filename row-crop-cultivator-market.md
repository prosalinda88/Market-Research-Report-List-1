<p><ul><li>Mounted Row Crop Cultivator</li><li>Trailed Row Crop Cultivator</li><li>Semi-mounted Row Crop Cultivator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Row crop cultivators are agricultural machines used for weed control and soil cultivation in row-cropped fields. There are three main types of row crop cultivators: mounted, trailed, and semi-mounted. Mounted cultivators are attached directly to the tractor's three-point hitch. Trailed cultivators are towed behind the tractor and are typically larger in size. Semi-mounted cultivators are partially attached to the tractor and partly supported by their own wheels. These different types offer farmers flexibility in terms of size, maneuverability, and ease of use, allowing them to select the most suitable cultivator for their specific field conditions.</p></p>
