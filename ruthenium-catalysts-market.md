<p><p>Ruthenium catalysts are available in two main types: powder and particle. Powder catalysts consist of finely ground ruthenium particles, which provide a large surface area for catalytic reactions. They are commonly used in industrial processes and chemical synthesis. On the other hand, particle catalysts are composed of larger-sized ruthenium particles embedded in a support material. These catalysts are particularly useful for applications that require better control over reaction rates and selectivity. Both types of catalysts have unique properties that make them suitable for specific catalytic reactions.</p></p>
