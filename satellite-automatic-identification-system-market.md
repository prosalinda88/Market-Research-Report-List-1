<p><p>The Satellite Automatic Identification System (S-AIS) market comprises two main types of transponders: Class A and Class B. Class A transponders are typically used by larger vessels, such as commercial ships, and they provide more detailed information about the vessel's identification, position, speed, and course. On the other hand, Class B transponders are commonly utilized by smaller vessels, like fishing boats or leisure craft, and offer less comprehensive data. Both transponder types facilitate the transmission of vessel information via satellite, aiding in maritime safety, collision avoidance, and vessel tracking.</p></p>
