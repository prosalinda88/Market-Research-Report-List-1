<p><p>Sealants are used to fill gaps and prevent the passage of fluids in various industries. There are different types of sealants available in the market. Water-borne sealants are made from water-based emulsions and are eco-friendly. Solvent-borne sealants are based on organic solvents and provide strong adhesion. Reactive sealants form a strong bond when exposed to moisture or air. Hot-melts are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, providing quick and easy application. Each type of sealant has its own advantages and is suited for specific applications.</p></p>
