<p><p>Self-adhesive switchable smart film is a high-tech laminated glass film that offers privacy on demand. The increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions and smart glass technologies in various end-user industries, such as healthcare, construction, automotive, and transportation, is driving the market's growth.
