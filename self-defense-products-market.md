<p><p>Self-defense products refer to items that are designed to protect individuals from potential threats or attacks. Firstly, there has been a transition from traditional self-defense tools to advanced and discrete devices. For instance, the development of smart self-defense devices, such as personal safety wearables and smartphone-connected alarms, has gained traction.
