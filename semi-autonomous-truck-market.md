<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Semi-Autonomous Truck market?</strong></p>
<p><p>The global semi-autonomous truck market is experiencing several emerging trends. Firstly, there is a growing focus on truck platooning, where multiple trucks travel closely together, reducing wind resistance and optimizing fuel efficiency. Additionally, the adoption of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) is increasing, providing features like adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assistance. Artificial intelligence and machine learning are being integrated into semi-autonomous trucks for better decision-making capabilities. The use of connected technologies, such as vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication, is gaining prominence. Lastly, the market is witnessing the development of sustainable and electric semi-autonomous trucks to address environmental concerns.</p></p>
