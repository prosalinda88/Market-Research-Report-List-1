<p><p>Semiconductor testing boards, also known as PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards), are crucial components in the semiconductor industry used for testing and troubleshooting electronic devices. There are three main types of semiconductor testing boards. Probe cards enable electrical connection between the device under test and the tester, allowing for precise measurements. Load boards are used to simulate and apply specific loads to the device, testing its performance under various conditions. Finally, burn-in boards subject the device to extreme temperatures, voltage, and power to ensure its reliability and detect potential failures before final use.</p></p>
