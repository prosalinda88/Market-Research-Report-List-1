<p><p>Shared electric bicycles, also known as e-bikes, are bicycles equipped with an electric motor that assists the rider when pedaling. Increased focus on lightweight and foldable e-bikes: To cater to the growing demand for portable and easy-to-store e-bikes, manufacturers are focusing on producing lightweight and foldable models.
