<p><p>The silencers market comprises three main types: rectangular silencers, elbow silencers, and circular silencers. Rectangular silencers are designed to minimize noise in rectangular duct systems and are commonly used in HVAC systems. Elbow silencers are specifically crafted for reducing noise in ventilation and air conditioning systems' elbow sections. Circular silencers are designed to attenuate noise in circular ductwork, commonly found in industrial and commercial settings. Each type serves its unique purpose in reducing noise levels and creating a more comfortable and peaceful environment.</p></p>
