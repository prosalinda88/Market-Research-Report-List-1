<p><p>The silicon organic water repellent market is categorized into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based silicon organic water repellent is a formulation where water is used as a diluent instead of solvent, making it more eco-friendly and less harmful to human health. Solvent-based silicon organic water repellent, on the other hand, utilizes organic solvents as diluents. While it may offer certain advantages in terms of application, it can be more harmful to the environment and human health due to the release of volatile organic compounds (VOCs).</p></p>
