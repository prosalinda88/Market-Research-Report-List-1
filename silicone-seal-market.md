<p><strong>The Silicone Seal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Radiation Curing</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanizing</li><li>Thermoset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone seal market can be classified into four types based on their curing processes. Pressure sensitive silicone seals adhere to surfaces under slight pressure, radiation curing seals are cured by exposure to UV or electron radiation, room temperature vulcanizing seals cure at ambient temperatures without requiring heat, and thermoset seals undergo a chemical reaction when heated and become permanently cured. These different types cater to various industries and applications, providing versatile solutions for sealing and bonding needs.</p></p>
