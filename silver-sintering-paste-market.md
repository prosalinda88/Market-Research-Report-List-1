<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Silver Sintering Paste market can be classified into two main types: Pressure Sintering and Pressure-less Sintering. Pressure Sintering involves applying pressure during the sintering process, which helps to enhance the bonding between particles and achieve a denser structure. Conversely, Pressure-less Sintering does not require any external pressure and relies on the diffusion of atoms to form strong bonds between particles. Both methods are widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and telecommunications for applications like die-attach and power module packaging.</p></p>
