<p><ul><li>Wide-slotted Wire Duct</li><li>Narrow-slotted Wire Duct</li><li>Round hole Wire Duct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slotted wire duct market can be divided into three types: wide-slotted wire duct, narrow-slotted wire duct, and round hole wire duct. Wide-slotted wire ducts have larger slots that provide more space for wires to fit and are commonly used for larger cables. Narrow-slotted wire ducts have thinner slots, making them suitable for smaller wires and cables. Round hole wire ducts have circular holes instead of slots, allowing for easy routing and organization of wires. These different types cater to various wire sizes and provide flexibility for different wire management needs.</p></p>
