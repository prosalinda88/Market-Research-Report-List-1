<p><p>Smart collar market can be divided into two types: GPS-based and radio-based. GPS-based smart collars utilize GPS technology to track the location of the pets wearing them. They provide real-time tracking and allow pet owners to monitor their pets remotely. On the other hand, radio-based smart collars use radio frequency signals for tracking. They have a limited range and are usually used for training purposes within a specific area or range. Both types of collars offer different functionalities and cater to specific needs of pet owners in terms of tracking and monitoring their pets.</p></p>
