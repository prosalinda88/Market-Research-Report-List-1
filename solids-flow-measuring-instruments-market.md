<p><strong>The Solids Flow Measuring Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contacting Measuring Instruments</li><li>Contactless Measuring Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solids flow measuring instruments market includes two main types: contacting measuring instruments and contactless measuring instruments. Contacting measuring instruments require physical contact with the solid material to measure its flow rate or velocity, typically using sensors or probes. Contactless measuring instruments, on the other hand, can measure the flow rate or velocity of solids without physical contact. They rely on technologies such as ultrasound, microwaves, or lasers to detect and measure the movement of solid materials. Both types of instruments play a crucial role in industries where accurate measurement of solids flow is essential.</p></p>
