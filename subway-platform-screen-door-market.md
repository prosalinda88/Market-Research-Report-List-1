<p><p>The subway platform screen door market consists of three main types: full-closed, semi-closed, and half height. Full-closed platform screen doors completely enclose the platform, ensuring passengers' safety by preventing any unauthorized access. Semi-closed platform screen doors partially enclose the platform, offering both safety and visibility. Half height platform screen doors only cover the lower portion of the platform, providing a cost-effective option while still ensuring passenger safety. These different types cater to varying transportation needs and budgets, offering flexible solutions for subway station operators.</p></p>
