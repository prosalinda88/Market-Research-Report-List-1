<p><p>Switchable privacy film is a technology that allows the glass to switch from transparent to opaque, providing privacy when needed. There are two main types of switchable privacy films: non-adhesive film and self-adhesive film. Non-adhesive film does not require any glue or adhesive to be applied to the glass surface and can be easily removed and reused. On the other hand, self-adhesive film has a sticky surface that adheres directly to the glass, providing a long-lasting and permanent solution for privacy needs. Both types of films offer the same privacy benefits but differ in their installation and removal methods.</p></p>
