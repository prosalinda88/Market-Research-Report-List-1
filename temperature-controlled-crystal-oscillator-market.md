<p><p>The Temperature Controlled Crystal Oscillator (TCXO) market consists of various types of oscillators, including Clipped Sine, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), HCMOS (High-Speed CMOS), Sine, and others. Sine TCXOs produce sine-wave output, which is ideal for analog systems.
