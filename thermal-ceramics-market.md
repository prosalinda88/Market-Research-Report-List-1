<p><p>The thermal ceramics market consists of two main types: ceramic fibers and insulation bricks. Ceramic fibers are lightweight and have high thermal resistance, making them suitable for various applications such as aerospace, automotive, and insulation in buildings. Insulation bricks, on the other hand, are dense and durable, providing excellent insulation properties. They are commonly used in industrial furnaces, kilns, and other high-temperature environments. Both ceramic fibers and insulation bricks play a crucial role in maintaining heat resistance and energy efficiency in different sectors.</p></p>
