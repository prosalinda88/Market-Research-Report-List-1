<p><p>Thermoformed skin packaging refers to a packaging technique where a plastic film is heated, stretched, and placed over a product before vacuum sealing it to a backing board. There are two types of thermoformed skin packaging: non-carded and carded. Non-carded thermoformed skin packaging does not involve the use of a backing board, meaning the product is directly sealed to the plastic film. In contrast, carded thermoformed skin packaging involves placing the product on a backing card before sealing it with the plastic film.</p></p>
