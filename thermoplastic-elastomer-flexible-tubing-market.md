<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomer flexible tubing is available in two main types: double tube type and multi-tube type. </p><p>Double tube type refers to a flexible tubing design that consists of two tubes, typically of different sizes or materials, encased within each other. This design allows for the simultaneous conveyance of multiple fluids or gases while maintaining separation.</p><p>On the other hand, multi-tube type refers to a flexible tubing design that features multiple tubes arranged parallel to each other. This design allows for the conveyance of multiple fluids or gases separately within the same tubing system.</p><p>Both double tube and multi-tube types offer versatility and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
