The construction industry is also witnessing growth globally, leading to increased usage of TPU nonwoven fabric for applications like roofing, insulation, and wall coverings.</p><p>Additionally, the medical industry is increasingly adopting TPU nonwoven fabric for disposable medical supplies, wound dressings, and surgical gowns, owing to its biocompatibility and antimicrobial properties. The rise in healthcare expenditure and the growing aging population are projected to fuel the demand for TPU nonwoven fabric in the medical sector.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on sustainability and eco-friendly materials is expected to drive the market growth.
