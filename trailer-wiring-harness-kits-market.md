Plugs are the connectors that allow trailer lights to be synchronized with the towing vehicle. Sockets are the receiving ends that connect to the plugs, ensuring a secure connection. These components play crucial roles in enabling a safe and effective transmission of electrical signals between the towing vehicle and trailer.</p></p>
