<p><p>The transparent conductive panel market includes two main types: glass and metallic oxide. Glass panels are made from a transparent material that allows light to pass through, while simultaneously conducting electricity. These panels are commonly used in touchscreens and display devices. Metallic oxide panels, on the other hand, are created by depositing a thin layer of metal oxide onto a substrate, enabling it to conduct electricity while maintaining transparency. These panels have applications in various industries, including electronics, solar panels, and automotive displays.</p></p>
