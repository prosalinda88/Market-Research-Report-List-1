<p><p>Vehicle video recorders, also known as dash cams or car cameras, are electronic devices that are installed in vehicles to record videos while driving. Manufacturers are integrating connectivity features like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth to enable easy transfer of video footage to smartphones or other devices.
