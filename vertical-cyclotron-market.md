<p><p>A Vertical Cyclotron is a type of particle accelerator used in nuclear physics research and medical applications. The demand for particle accelerators is expected to increase in various fields, such as nuclear medicine, cancer therapy, and fundamental research. The increased use of cyclotrons in radiopharmaceutical production, especially for Positron Emission Tomography (PET) imaging, is a major driver for market growth.</p><p>The medical sector is a significant contributor to the vertical cyclotron market. Cyclotrons are used for the production of radioisotopes, which are an essential component of nuclear medicine. These radioisotopes are used for diagnostic imaging and therapeutic treatments. With the increasing prevalence of cancer and other diseases, the demand for radioisotopes is expected to rise, thereby driving the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology, such as the development of compact and efficient cyclotrons, are also contributing to the market's growth potential.
