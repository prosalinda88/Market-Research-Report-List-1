<p><p>The VPH (Volume Phase Holographic) grating market consists of two main types: Enhanced-Volume Phase Holographic Gratings (EVPHGs) and HOEs (Holographic Optical Elements). EVPHGs are high-performance gratings that offer improved efficiency and low scattering, making them suitable for applications like spectroscopy and telecommunications. On the other hand, HOEs are holographic optical elements that utilize VPH technology to create diffractive optical elements, enabling applications in areas such as display systems, laser beam shaping, and optical storage. Both EVPHGs and HOEs play crucial roles in various industries, offering enhanced optical functionalities and performance.</p></p>
