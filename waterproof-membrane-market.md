<p><p>The waterproof membrane market encompasses various types such as Modified Bitumen, PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). Modified Bitumen membranes are made from asphalt, while PVC membranes are synthetic and highly durable. EPDM membranes, made from rubber, offer excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and UV radiation. TPO membranes are a blend of polypropylene and ethylene-propylene rubber, providing superior weather resistance. HDPE and LDPE membranes, both made of polyethylene, offer flexibility and resistance to chemicals.</p></p>
