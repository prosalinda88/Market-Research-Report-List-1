<p><p>Weatherstrip coatings market can be classified into two types: single component and double component. Single component coatings are ready-to-use coatings that do not require any additional mixing or blending before application. They offer convenience and ease of use. On the other hand, double component coatings require the combination of separate resin and hardener components in a specific ratio before application. These coatings provide better durability and performance, as the chemical reaction between the components enhances their properties.</p></p>
