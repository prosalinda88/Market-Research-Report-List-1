<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles have two main market types: dynamic wireless charging systems and stationary wireless charging systems. Dynamic wireless charging systems enable charging while the vehicle is in motion, using charging coils placed under the road surface. This allows for continuous charging during the journey. On the other hand, stationary wireless charging systems require the vehicle to be parked over a wireless charging pad for recharging. These systems are typically used in parking lots or garages, providing convenient and efficient charging options for electric vehicles.</p></p>
